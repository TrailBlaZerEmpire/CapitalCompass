About CapitalCompass

CapitalCompass is a personal financial initiative focused on options strategies and equity hedging, with an emphasis on risk-neutral positioning.Rather than chasing quick wins, this project helps build a grounded, snowballing mindset around capital management.

This space documents growth journeys — trade reflections, fund milestones, and mental models — centered on disciplined, calculated risk. It's not about speculation, but about building an inner framework that aligns with sustainable portfolio resilience.

CapitalCompass is part of TrailBlazer Empire, a larger vision spanning business strategy (VisionCraft) and semiconductor technology (SiliconBridge). Together, these units aim to bridge personal ambition with global ecosystems.

Founder: Rheeshaalaen Sabapathy
Location: Malaysia, Asia-Pacific
Mission: To guide individuals and businesses from confusion to clarity, through structured thinking and ethical decision-making.
